How long does company formation take in Bahrain?

For a standard W.L.L., 2 to 4 weeks from name reservation to a live CR — assuming all shareholder documents are in order. Specialized activities (financial services, education, healthcare) require additional sectoral approvals and run 6 to 12 weeks.

Do I need to be physically present?

Not for most steps. We act under power of attorney for signings, ministry submissions, and bank account opening preliminaries. A short visit is typically required for biometrics or the final bank meeting.

What does setup actually cost?

Government fees are fixed by activity. Our fees are flat — quoted before we begin, never billed by the hour. A simple W.L.L. starts at BD 750 plus government charges; capital-market work is scoped per engagement.

Are there ongoing costs after incorporation?

Annual CR renewal, audit if your activity requires it, EWA, GOSI for staff, and VAT registration above the threshold. We hand you a maintenance calendar at handover.

What is a 'Professional Body' under MOIC?

A consultancy formally recognized by the Ministry of Industry & Commerce to submit, amend, and execute commercial registry matters on behalf of clients — مكتب معتمد. It means our submissions are treated as authorized rather than third-party.

Can foreigners own 100% of a Bahraini company?

For most activities, yes. A growing list of sectors permits full foreign ownership; a small list still requires a Bahraini partner. We confirm the answer for your specific activity before you commit.

Do you help with bank account opening?

Yes. We prepare KYC packs, introduce you to relationship managers at banks we work with regularly, and accompany you to the account-opening meeting.

Can you handle ongoing corporate secretarial work?

Yes. Board minutes, AGM/EGM coordination, registry changes, annual returns, and CBB filings are part of our corporate services retainer.
